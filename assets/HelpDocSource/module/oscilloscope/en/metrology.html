Measure

On Interface Guide introduced D area, find [Measure], click it to enter menu options.

It is mainly used to measure the wave form. Steps include selection of signal source and wave form measurement.

Enter [Measure] menu, click [Signal Source] to select signal source.

Enter [Measure] menu, click [ Horizontal Measure] or [Vertical Measure] to perform cursor measurement to wave form. Cursor measurement obtains data through the movement of A-line and B-line, see below image, the data displays on the area marked by green line .

Please click [Clear Measure] to exit the measurement.
